About me

I am currently working at Zenexity. Previously, I have worked as product architect at Obeo. In 4 years we created an innovative modeling workbench, contributing significantly to open-source. Before, I notably worked at IBM Zurich Research Laboratory on data access enhancement for wireless sensor networks with MQTT and at the French Institute of Athens on several communication actions.

I have graduated both Telecom Lille 1, an engineering grande ecole, and LIFL, the Computer Science Laboratory of Lille.
